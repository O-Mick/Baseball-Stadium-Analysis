# Baseball-Stadium-Analysis
Studying different offensivestatistics in Major league ball parks over the 2025 season. 

Basic analysis to look at Batting averages and home runs hit in different stadiums
Attempting to make clean visuals for easy reading and Analysis!

Started off the project by importing statcast data for the 2025 season and creating a new data frame with events and teams.
I aggregated a list that included plate appearence events - home runs, hits, and at bats to home teams.

After that I sorted by the top 5 hits and home runs in each ballpark. I then created an ascending value of home runs over the season 
which was lead by Dodger Stadium (surprise, surprise) and then the same with batting average which was lead by Coors Field. Considering
factors like field size and elevation this is not very surprising.

Finally, I wanted to look at how these numbers for the top 5 stadiums progressed throughout the season. I grouped by home team and game date. Then cumulated home runs and Runs over the season
and wanted visualized how these numbers increased over the season.
